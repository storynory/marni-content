---
title: Roasted Veg & Mozzarella Toastie
date: 2025-11-20T12:10:00Z
thumbnail: /uploads/newsandwch.webp
ingredients:
  - 2 slices sourdough
  - ½ courgette, sliced and roasted
  - ½ red pepper, roasted
  - 80 g mozzarella
  - 1 tsp pesto
instructions:
  - Spread pesto on one slice of bread.
  - Layer roasted vegetables and mozzarella.
  - Top with the other slice and toast in a pan until golden.
methodNotes: A drizzle of balsamic makes it even better.
