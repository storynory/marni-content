ingredients:
  - 150 g ricotta
  - 1 lemon, zest
  - 1 egg
  - 120 ml milk
  - 100 g flour
  - 1 tsp baking powder
instructions:
  - Whisk ricotta, egg, milk and zest.
  - Fold in flour and baking powder.
  - Fry small rounds until lightly coloured.
methodNotes: Soft, bright and perfect for Easter mornings.
