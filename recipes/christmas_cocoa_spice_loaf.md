---
title: Cocoa Spice Loaf
date: 2025-11-27T08:00:00Z
thumbnail: /uploads/Cocoa_Spice_Loaf_.webp
ingredients:
  - 180 g plain flour
  - 30 g cocoa powder
  - 120 g soft brown sugar
  - 2 eggs
  - 80 ml oil
  - 1 tsp baking powder
  - 1 tsp cinnamon
  - ½ tsp ginger
instructions:
  - Whisk eggs, oil and sugar.
  - Add dry ingredients and stir.
  - Bake at 170°C for 40–45 minutes.
methodNotes: Great toasted with butter.
