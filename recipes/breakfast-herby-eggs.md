---
title: Herby Eggs with Spinach & Rye
date: 2025-11-20T08:10:00Z
thumbnail: /uploads/herby-eggs-rides-again.webp
ingredients:
  - 2 large eggs
  - A handful of spinach
  - 1 tbsp chopped herbs (parsley or dill)
  - 1 tbsp butter
  - 2 slices rye toast
instructions:
  - Melt the butter in a pan.
  - Add spinach and wilt briefly.
  - Crack in the eggs and scramble gently.
  - Stir through herbs and spoon over rye toast.
methodNotes: Works beautifully with soft goat's cheese crumbled on top.
