---
title: Spiced Cranberry Buns
date: 2025-11-25T08:00:00Z
thumbnail: /uploads/Spiced_Cranberry_Buns.webp
ingredients:
  - 300 g strong white flour
  - 50 g soft butter
  - 150 ml warm milk
  - 1 egg
  - 60 g dried cranberries
  - 40 g sugar
  - 1 tsp cinnamon
  - 1 tsp mixed spice
  - 1 tsp fast‑action yeast
instructions:
  - Mix flour, sugar, spices and yeast.
  - Add milk, butter and egg; knead to a soft dough.
  - Fold in cranberries.
  - Rise 1 hour, shape into buns, rise again.
  - Bake at 180°C for 18–20 minutes.
methodNotes: Lovely warm with butter.
