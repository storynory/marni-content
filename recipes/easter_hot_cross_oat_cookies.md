---
title: Hot Cross Oat Cookies
date: 2026-03-21T08:00:00Z
thumbnail: /uploads/Hot_Cross_Oat_Cookies.webp
ingredients:
  - 120 g oats
  - 100 g flour
  - 80 g butter
  - 80 g sugar
  - 1 tsp mixed spice
  - 40 g raisins
instructions:
  - Cream butter and sugar.
  - Add dry ingredients and raisins.
  - Spoon onto tray; bake at 180°C for 12–14 minutes.
methodNotes: A quick Easter treat with familiar flavours.
