---
title: Minced Apple Crumble Slice
date: 2025-11-26T08:00:00Z
thumbnail: /uploads/minced-apple-crumble.jpg
ingredients:
  - 2 apples, grated
  - 150 g mincemeat
  - 180 g plain flour
  - 120 g butter
  - 80 g brown sugar
  - Pinch of salt
instructions:
  - Rub butter into flour, sugar and salt to make crumbs.
  - Press two‑thirds into a lined tin.
  - Mix apples with mincemeat and spread over.
  - Scatter remaining crumbs and bake at 170°C for 30 minutes.
methodNotes: A gentle twist on mince pies.
