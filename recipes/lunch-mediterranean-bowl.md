---
title: Mediterranean Couscous Bowl
date: 2025-11-20T12:00:00Z
thumbnail: /uploads/cous-cous.webp
ingredients:
  - 120 g couscous
  - 200 ml hot stock
  - 1 handful cherry tomatoes, halved
  - 1 small cucumber, diced
  - 50 g feta cheese, crumbled
  - Olive oil, lemon juice, salt
instructions:
  - Place couscous in a bowl and pour over hot stock.
  - Cover and leave 5 minutes, then fluff with a fork.
  - Add tomatoes, cucumber and feta.
  - Dress with olive oil and lemon juice.
methodNotes: Add olives or toasted seeds for extra texture.
