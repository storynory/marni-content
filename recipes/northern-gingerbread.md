---
title: Northern Gingerbread (Modern Healthy Version)
date: 2025-12-01
thumbnail: /uploads/northern-ginger-bread-house.webp
ingredients:
  - Wholemeal Flour
  - Rye Flour
  - Rolled Oats
  - Bicarbonate of Soda
  - Ground Ginger
  - Ground Cinnamon
  - Sea Salt
  - Black Treacle
  - Honey
  - Unsalted Butter
  - Eggs
  - Natural Yoghurt
instructions:
  - Heat the oven to 160°C (fan) and line a 20 × 20 cm tin with baking parchment.
  - In a large bowl mix the wholemeal flour, rye flour, oats, bicarbonate of soda, ginger, cinnamon and salt.
  - Gently warm the treacle, honey and butter in a pan until just melted.
  - Whisk the eggs and yoghurt in a jug, then pour in the warm treacle mixture.
  - Stir the wet ingredients into the dry until smooth and glossy.
  - Pour into the tin and bake for 30–35 minutes, or until risen and springy.
  - Cool completely to let the ginger develop its flavour.

A softer, more wholesome twist on northern gingerbread. The blend of rye and oats gives a lovely chew, while the treacle and honey keep it rich without being too sweet. It improves after a day or two in a tin.
