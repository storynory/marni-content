---
title: Ginger Pear Crumble
date: 2025-11-28T08:00:00Z
thumbnail: /uploads/ginger-crumble.webp
ingredients:
  - 4 pears, sliced
  - 1 tbsp stem ginger syrup
  - 150 g flour
  - 80 g butter
  - 60 g oats
  - 60 g sugar
instructions:
  - Toss pears with ginger syrup and place in a dish.
  - Rub butter into flour, oats and sugar.
  - Scatter crumble and bake at 180°C for 25–30 minutes.
methodNotes: Perfumed, mellow and cosy.
