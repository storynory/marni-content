thumbnail: /uploads/new-choc-mouse.webp
ingredients:
  - 2 ripe avocados
  - 3 tbsp cocoa powder
  - 2–3 tbsp maple syrup
  - 1 tsp vanilla extract
instructions:
  - Blend avocados, cocoa, maple syrup and vanilla until smooth.
  - Chill for 20 minutes before serving.
methodNotes: Surprisingly creamy and rich — no dairy needed.
