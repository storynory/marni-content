---
title: Lemon & Herb Roast Chicken Thighs
date: 2025-11-20T18:10:00Z
thumbnail: /uploads/chicken.webp
ingredients:
  - 4 chicken thighs
  - Juice of 1 lemon
  - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tsp dried oregano
  - Salt and pepper
instructions:
  - Mix lemon, oil, oregano, salt and pepper.
  - Coat chicken and marinate 20 minutes.
  - Roast at 200°C for 30–35 minutes.
methodNotes: Add potatoes to roast in the same tray.
